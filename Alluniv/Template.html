About ABC

ABC University is India's best engineering institute. For the past five years, the NIRF has ranked ABC University at the first position. The institute has 17 departments, 14 research centres, and a Continuing Education Programs Centre (CEP).
All of the centres offer 121 courses in Engineering, Science, Humanities, and Management. ABC University has signed MOUs with a number of foreign universities for student exchange programs. As per the MoUs signed, students pursuing UG, PG, and Dual Degree programs at the institute spend a semester abroad for broad exposure. In addition, the institute also offers Joint Doctoral Programs (JDPs) in collaboration with 17 foreign universities. The institute also provides an AICTE-sponsored Quality Improvement Program (QIP). Out of the 121 courses offered by ABC University, BTech and Online Degree program has the highest popularity. BTech is the flagship program of the institute. IIT Madras BTech

The BTech program at IIT Madras is placed in the first position by the NIRF. The program is specialized in 9 streams. The total intake capacity of the IIT Madras BTech program is 877 seats. The per semester fee for the program is INR 1,00,550. The BTech program gives a good return on investment. For the INR 8.08 Lacs of the fee for the whole program, the students get an average salary package of INR 20 LPA in the placement process. The most opted branch of the BTech program is the CSE. 

The cutoff for the IIT Madras CSE is highest among all the streams. The cutoff rank for the course is 85-163 in the JEE Advanced exam. The placement rate of the CSE branch is 84%. Students pursuing the CSE branch receive a higher package of INR 70 LPA, an average salary package of INR 25 LPA, and the lowest salary of INR 9 LPA. IIT Madras 2-year MTech program is specialized in 35 streams. Admission to the M.Tech program is based on the 500-700 GATE scores followed by a written test and personal interview conducted by the institute. IIT graduates who have a CGPA of 8.0 and above can also apply without GATE scores. IIT Madras PhD

IIT Madras PhD is a 5 year program currently offered in 5 departments, namely, Engineering, Science, Humanities, Social Sciences, and Management. For admission to the research program, candidates with 60% aggregate marks, a Master’s degree in a relevant field, and a valid GATE or UGC-JRF/NET/CSIR-JRF score can apply for admission. Candidates with a master’s degree from IITs with a CGPA of 8.0 and above can apply directly for admission. However, the final selection of the candidate for the program is done only after the interview round conducted by the institute.Direct admission to PhD program at IIT Madras



IIT Madras Online Courses

The Indian Institute of Technology, Madras has introduced a substitute for Online Degrees. The substitute allows students to study for an undergraduate degree or diploma anywhere in India. The program has no limits on age and academic background. The programs offered under the IIT Madras Online Courses/ Degree include Foundation + Diploma in Programming or Foundation + Diploma in Data Science and B.Sc in Programming and Data Science. The basic eligibility criteria for these programs require a student to have studied Mathematics and English in their 10 and should have previously/ currently enrolled in any of the bachelor's degree programs or diplomas.

The interested students can enroll in the online courses either via regular mode or diploma-only entry. Students registering via regular mode are required to join at the Foundation level, which consists of 8 courses. Students registering at the Diploma Only Entry cannot proceed with their diploma towards the BSc degree.

IIT Madras Scholarships 

To encourage meritorious students and help needy students with financial support, IIT Madras offers different types of scholarships and fee waivers. There are several schemes available for financial assistance by alumni, companies, Govt. of India, and institute at IITM. The following scholarships are famous among IITM students.

  1. Institute Merit-cum-Means Scholarships: IITM offers up to 67% tuition fee exemption to only 25% of students admitted. Students whose annual parental income is less than INR 4.5 Lakhs are eligible to apply for MCM scholarships. Also, the subject of renewing this scholarship is a minimum GPA of 5.0 in the previous semester.
  2. Institute Free Studentship: Likewise MCM scholarship, IIT Madras offers a free studentship for the students whose annual parental income is less than INR 4,50,000 lakhs. Students are exempted from the payment of 67% of the tuition fee under this scheme. However, the scholarship is only offered to 25% of the UG students.
  3. Institute Notional Prize:  On the basis of the JEE rank and annual parental income not exceeding INR 4,50,000, IITM offers a one-time grant of INR 1000 to the 7% of the students admitted.
  4. SC/ST Scholarship: IITM offers renewable institute SC/ST scholarship especially for the students belonging from SC/ST categories whose annual parental income doesn’t exceed INR 4,50,000. Students under this scheme are offered tuition fee exemption along with a free messing and monthly pocket allowance of INR 250.
